About Picnic

Picnic is an online supermarket that delivers all your groceries to your home for free. In the Picnic app you will find exactly the same groceries as in the supermarket, but for the lowest price. Picnic was founded in 2015 by entrepreneurs Joris Beckers, Frederik Nieuwenhuys, Michiel Muller and Bas Verheijen. Gerard Scheij also came on board later.

In September 2015, Picnic started in Amersfoort The Netherlands with four delivery vehicles. There are now about 800 cars driving around in more than 70 Dutch towns. With hundreds of thousands of customers and a monthly expansion to new cities, Picnic was named the fastest growing company in the Netherlands in 2019.

How it works

Picnic aims to take gocery shopping almost completely to the internet. In a time where we can order almost anything online why not order your groceries from the comfort of your own home as well?

At Picnic you can order your groceries via our mobile app. When submitting your order you will supply the location where you would like your groceries delivered. Much like ordering other things online you will receive a notification of when your groceries would be delivered at your doorstep.
